Recent Projects

Project 1

Resume Generator

A front-end based resume generator website is a platform that allows users to create and customize their professional resumes online with a user-friendly and visually appealing interface. Users can input their personal and professional information, select from various templates, and format their resumes without the need for advanced design or coding skills. This web application focuses on the user interface and user experience, making it easy for individuals to craft polished resumes that can help them stand out in the job market. Once the resume is created, users can typically download it in various formats (e.g., PDF or Word) to share with potential employers.

Project 2

Food Order System

A food order system is an online or mobile application that enables customers to browse, select, and place orders for food items from restaurants or eateries. It typically includes menus, item descriptions, pricing, and sometimes images. Customers can customize their orders, specify preferences, and pay electronically. The system streamlines the food ordering process, provides order tracking, and may offer delivery or pickup options. For restaurants, it facilitates order management, enhances customer engagement, and may include features like order history, loyalty programs, and feedback collection, improving overall efficiency and customer satisfaction in the food service industry.

Project 3

Weather App

A Weather App website project is a platform that allows users to access up-to-date weather information for various locations. Users can input a city or region, and the website provides current weather conditions, temperature, humidity, wind speed, and forecasts. This web-based application offers a user-friendly interface for quickly checking the weather, helping individuals plan their daily activities or travel with ease. Additionally, it may include additional features like weather maps, radar images, or weather alerts for severe conditions, enhancing its utility for users.
